🔄 2. DETAILED PROCESS FLOW
🔹 MASTER FLOW
START
 ↓
User taps "Start Checkup"
 ↓
AI Symptom Intake (Voice/Text)
 ↓
AI generates structured summary + risk level
 ↓
Vitals captured (SpO2, HR, BP)
 ↓
Display combined report
 ↓
Prompt: "Consult Doctor?"
 ↓
IF YES → Initiate Video Call
 ↓
Doctor views data + interacts
 ↓
Prescription / Advice
 ↓
END
🔹 AI TRIAGE FLOW (Agent Logic)
Input: Raw user speech/text
 ↓
Speech-to-Text (if voice)
 ↓
Symptom Extraction
 ↓
Keyword + Intent Mapping
 ↓
Risk Scoring Engine
 ↓
Output:
  - Symptoms list
  - Duration (if captured)
  - Risk: Low / Medium / High
🔹 DOCTOR FLOW
Doctor logs into dashboard
 ↓
Sees incoming consultation request
 ↓
Accept call
 ↓
Views:
  - Patient summary
  - AI triage output
  - Live vitals
 ↓
Consults via video
 ↓
Uploads prescription / notes
 ↓
Session ends
